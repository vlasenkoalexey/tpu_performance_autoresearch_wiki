# Qwen3 JAX current v102 - 2k batch17 Splash BKV2048 shmem90

## Hypothesis under test

**Hypothesis**: v101 did not beat the batch16 BKV2048 speed frontier, but it
cut runtime HBM from the tight **~0.10 GiB free** v095/v096/v097 band to
**2.002 GiB free** while keeping the same optimized train-step HLO. That
headroom may let the adjacent **batch17** shape fit with BKV2048 and improve
tokens/sec enough to beat the confirmed v095/v096 **70.95k tok/s** band.

**Mechanism**: Reuse v101 exactly and change only:

```bash
--batch_size=17
```

Keep `--seqlen=2048`, scoped VMEM **98304 KiB**, shmem90, no-scan remat,
activation sharding, Tokamax Splash, MaxText CE, SparseCore reduce-scatter and
all-reduce offload, SparseCore collective aggregator, and:

```bash
export SPLASH_BKV=2048
export SPLASH_BKV_COMPUTE=2048
```

**Predicted signal**: Support requires clean finite loss plus throughput above
the confirmed v095/v096 **70.95k tok/s** band. Runtime OOM, compile OOM, invalid
numerics, or throughput at/below the v090/v091 batch17 non-BKV2048 band refutes
batch17+BKV2048.

Refuted. shmem90 provided enough headroom for batch17+BKV2048 to fit, but the
larger batch increased profiled step time to **3963.8 ms** and throughput landed
below the batch16 frontier. Do not carry batch17+BKV2048.
