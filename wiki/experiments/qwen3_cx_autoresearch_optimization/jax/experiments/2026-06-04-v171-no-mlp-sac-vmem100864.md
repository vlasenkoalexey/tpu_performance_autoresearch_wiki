# Qwen3 JAX v171 - no MLP intermediate sac scoped VMEM 100864

## Hypothesis under test

**Hypothesis**: v169/v170 confirmed a new HLO family by removing only the MLP
intermediate `_sac` constraint. The previous 100864 KiB scoped-VMEM high-side
probe on the old HLO family was a same-HLO tie/noise, but the new layout may
have a different local VMEM optimum.

**Mechanism**: Keep the v169/v170 image and change only:

```text
--xla_tpu_scoped_vmem_limit_kib=100352 -> 100864
```

All code, batch/sequence shape, CE, attention, remat, scan, and other runtime
flags remain fixed.

**Predicted signal**: Support requires clean loss, HLO in the no-MLP-sac family
or a new faster schedule, and average throughput above the v169/v170 confirmed
frontier band (57,935-57,947 tok/s).

**Falsification criterion**: Compile/runtime failure, invalid loss, fallback to
the old v153 HLO family, or clean completion at/below v169/v170 without a
profile/HLO win.

## Verdict

**Refuted/tie.** Raising scoped VMEM from 100352 to 100864 KiB on the
v169/v170 no-MLP-intermediate-`_sac` HLO family did not select a new compiled
program and did not improve throughput. Keep the v169/v170 frontier and stop
probing nearby scoped-VMEM values in this HLO family.
