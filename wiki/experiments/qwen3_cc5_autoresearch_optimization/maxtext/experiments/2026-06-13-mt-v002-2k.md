[mt-v001](2026-06-12-mt-v001-base.md) recipe (llama3_1_8b_8192_no_collective_matmul

| TFLOP/s/device (their accounting) | 388.0 (≈42.3% of 918 peak) | — | |
| MFU (our accounting) | ≈40.7% | 40.5% | |
| HBM after init | **11.55 / 31.25 GiB (37%)** — host offload | 27.28 GiB (87%) at bs5 | |

Loss 12.43 → 1.218 smooth descent (synthetic). Their per-chip token load is
24,576 (bs12) vs our 10,240 (bs5) — at 2k both stacks sit on the flat part
of the batch curve; per-token cost is statistically tied.

**Observation**: MaxText's HBM is only 37% — their host-offload remat leaves
~20 GiB headroom, so their 2k batch ceiling is much higher than bs12.
Whether more batch helps them (their collective floor is already overlapped)
is an open probe (mt-v003 candidate, bs24) — needed before claiming a
final stack-vs-stack verdict at 2k.
