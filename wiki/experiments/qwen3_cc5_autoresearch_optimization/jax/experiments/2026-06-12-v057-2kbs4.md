# Qwen3-8B jax v057 — bs4 at 2k

First rung of the reopened 2k batch ladder:
[v055](2026-06-12-v055-2kstack.md)'s substrate (scan + save_qkv_ctx_hbm +
context + 21-flag stack) with `--batch_size=4`.

## Hypothesis under test

[qwen3-jax-2k-batch-rescale](../../../../hypotheses/qwen3-jax-2k-batch-rescale.md).

**Mechanism**: v055's 186 ms collective floor (ARS 146.5 + AG 26.6 + AR
13.0 ms) is weight-sized — fixed per step. Doubling tokens/step amortizes it
from 27.3% to ~14% while token-proportional buckets (~486 ms) scale 2×.

**Predicted signal**: step ≈ 1,100–1,250 ms (envelope 486×2+186 ≈ 1,158);
tok/s/chip ≈ 6,500–7,100. Peak HBM ≈ 25.4 GiB (~81%; v055 stack 9.83 GiB
scales ~linearly, heap 5.78 fixed). PASS = tok/s/chip > 6,320 (+3% over
v052's 6,136) with loss-series stability. OOM ⇒ the stack buffer scales
worse than linear — fall back to bs3.

## Results

20 steps clean (exit 0); compile ~92 s (scan fast-path); no OOM.

| Metric | v052 (bs2 unrolled) | v055 (bs2 same substrate) | **v057 (bs4)** | Δ vs v052 |
|--------|---------------------|---------------------------|----------------|-----------|
| Step (median steady) | 667.6 ms | 681.6 ms | **1,094.5 ms** | +64% (2× tokens) |
| tok/s/chip | 6,136 | 6,005 | **7,479** | **+21.9%** |
| MFU | ≈31.6% | 30.9% | **≈38.5%** | **+6.9 pp** |
| Loss (start → 19) | 12.0914 → 12.0794 | same | 12.0953 → 12.0659, stable desc. | ✓ (batch = throughput config) |

PASS bar (>6,320 tok/s/chip) cleared by +18.3%. Better than the +15%
envelope — the fixed 186 ms collective floor amortized as predicted AND MXU
efficiency rose with the larger per-step matmuls.
