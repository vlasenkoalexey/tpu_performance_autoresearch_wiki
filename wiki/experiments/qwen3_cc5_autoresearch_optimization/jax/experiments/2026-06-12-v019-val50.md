# Qwen3-8B jax v019 — 50-step validation of the final seq-8192 recipe

measurement (50 steps, profile late) and a long-horizon stability check.

## Hypothesis under test

**Hypothesis**: The v017 recipe (splash + chunked CE f32-x + save_attn remat
+ SKIP_EVERY=5 + vmem flag) holds 1390 ms / 47,146 tok/s / ≈33.8% MFU over
50 steps with no NaN and no step-time drift — confirming the frontier as a
durable measurement rather than a 20-step snapshot.

**Mechanism**: No change — the merged trunk recipe, run longer
(`--train_steps=50`, profile at steps 40–42).

**Predicted signal**: median steady step ≈ 1390 ms over steps 2–49; loss
monotone-noisy descent; no drift (step-time slope ~0).

**Falsification criterion**: NaN at any step, or median step time > 1390 +
1%, or visible upward drift → the 20-step frontier numbers get a caveat.
