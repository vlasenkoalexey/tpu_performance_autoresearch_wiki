# Qwen3-8B jax v059 — bs6 probe (measured ladder close)

Decisive close of the 2k batch ladder above the
[v058](2026-06-12-v058-2kbs5.md) frontier (7,874 tok/s/chip @ bs5). The
measured slope (4.10 GiB/bs from v055→v057, validated by v058 fitting)
projects bs6 ≈ 32.03 GiB = **102.5% of cap — OOM by ~0.78 GiB**. A ~5-min
compile-OOM probe converts the projection into a measured close (v039
precedent: knife-edge margins are data).

## Hypothesis under test

[qwen3-jax-2k-batch-rescale](../../../../hypotheses/qwen3-jax-2k-batch-rescale.md),
final rung.

**Predicted signal**: compile-time `RESOURCE_EXHAUSTED`, ~32 GiB used,
exceeded by ~0.8 GiB, scan-stacked residual buffers topping allocations ⇒
ladder closes at bs5 with a measured margin. Upset case: fits (XLA finds
~1 GiB of packing) AND > 7,953 tok/s/chip (+1% over v058) ⇒ bs6 is the rung.

## Results

**Compile-time OOM as projected — measured margin sharper than the
extrapolation**: `Used 31.82G of 31.25G hbm. Exceeded by 587.89M.`
(projection said ~0.78 GiB over; actual 0.57 GiB). 0 steps; ~5 min wall.

**Top allocations — the wall is CE, not remat:**

| # | Size | Shape | What |
|---|------|-------|------|
| 1 | **6.96 G** | `f32[12288,151936]` | log_softmax **gradient** (bs·seq/chip × vocab) |
| 2 | **6.96 G** | `f32[6,2048,151936]` | **forward logit buffer** (plain-CE f32 materialization) |
| 3 | 1.16 G | `bf16[151936,4096]` | lm_head weight grad RS |

The two f32 CE buffers = **13.9 GiB of the 26.1 GiB program footprint**,
scaling ~2.3 GiB/bs combined. Also notable: **42.2% fragmentation (11.02
GiB padding waste)** in the HLO temp accounting.

## Verdict

**refuted** (bs6 on the plain-CE substrate) — **the batch ladder is
measured-closed at bs5 for THIS substrate**, with a decisive twist: the
binding allocation is the f32 CE logit pair, not the remat residuals. The
non-CE activation slope is only ~1.8 GiB/bs — **chunked CE (which
eliminates the [BL,V] materialization) would reopen bs6 and plausibly bs8**.
The 2k-era chunked-CE refutation (+3.2% at bs2, v003-lineage) was measured
where it had nothing to unlock; at the ladder top it buys rungs worth ~+10%
each. Filed as
[qwen3-jax-2k-chunked-ce-batch-unlock](../../../../hypotheses/qwen3-jax-2k-chunked-ce-batch-unlock.md).
