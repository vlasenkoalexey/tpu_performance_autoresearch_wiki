# Qwen3-8B jax v061 — bs8 + chunked CE

The CE-unlock route past the certified bs5 frontier
([v060](2026-06-12-v060-2kbs5val.md): 7,871 tok/s/chip / 40.5% MFU):
`chunked_xla` CE removes the f32 logit pair that
[v059](2026-06-12-v059-2kbs6.md) measured as the bs6 wall (~13.9 GiB at bs6,
~2.3 GiB/bs), jumping straight to the win case **bs8**.

## Hypothesis under test

[qwen3-jax-2k-chunked-ce-batch-unlock](../../../../hypotheses/qwen3-jax-2k-chunked-ce-batch-unlock.md).

**Mechanism**: chunked CE streams the vocab dimension (no [BL,V]
materialization); non-CE activation slope is ~1.8 GiB/bs ⇒ bs8 ≈ 28 GiB
projected. 2× tokens vs bs4 amortize the ~192 ms collective floor further.

**Predicted signal**: fits; step ≈ 2,000–2,150 ms (compute ~227 ms/bs × 8 +
floor ~192 + CE overhead); tok/s/chip ≈ 7,800–8,400. PASS = tok/s/chip >
7,950 (+1% over the certified 7,871) AND loss series within ~1e-3 of the
plain-CE trajectory (12.0960/12.0969/12.0933 — the f32-x rule preserves
numerics; v008b tolerance). OOM ⇒ fall back bs6+CE. Fits-but-flat ⇒ CE
overhead at 2k eats the amortization — hypothesis refuted, bs5 stands.

## Results

20 steps clean (exit 0); **no OOM — the CE unlock worked** (bs8 fits where
plain-CE bs6 OOM'd). Steps 0/1 ~99 s each (chunked-CE autotune cache miss on
this cluster, both fwd and bwd kernels; one-time).

| Metric | v060 certified (bs5, plain CE) | **v061 (bs8, chunked CE)** | Δ |
|--------|--------------------------------|-----------------------------|---|
| Step (median steady) | 1,301 ms | **2,051.5 ms** | +57.7% (+60% tokens) |
| tok/s/chip | 7,871 | **7,987** | **+1.5%** |
| MFU | 40.5% | **≈41.1%** | +0.6 pp |
| Loss steps 0/1/2 | 12.0960 / 12.0969 / 12.0933 | 12.1039 / 12.0997 / 12.0980 | ≤7.9e-3 — see below |

PASS bar (>7,950) cleared. Loss-series note: the offset vs the bs5 reference
confounds **batch change (different sampled data at global 64 vs 40)** with
CE numerics — not interpretable as a CE-parity check.
[v062](2026-06-12-v062-2kce5par.md) (bs5 + chunked CE, same data as v058)
isolates CE; verdict finalizes on it.

## Verdict

**invalid** (finalized after the [v062](2026-06-12-v062-2kce5par.md) parity
arm) — the metric PASS (7,987 tok/s/chip, +1.5%) is **not reportable as a
win** per rule 8: same-data A/B shows chunked_xla CE carries a systematic
+0.006 loss offset vs plain CE
([observation](../../../../observations/chunked-ce-padded-vocab-loss-offset.md);
hypothesized padded-vocab phantom mass, ≈0.99× CE-grad scaling). The memory
mechanism itself was real (bs8 fits where plain-CE bs6 OOM'd) and the
batch-amortization arithmetic held — on a parity-clean CE kernel this route
re-opens for ≈ +1.5%. Note also the curve flattening: bs8 is the hard HBM
ceiling regardless.
