# Qwen3-8B jax v052 — 2k baseline on charles-v6e

Phase-opening run of the user-directed **seq-2048 optimization phase** on
the **charles-v6e** cluster: the historical 2k-best recipe
([v007](2026-06-12-v007-bs2-vmem.md)) verbatim on the new cluster + current
trunk image.

## Hypothesis under test

[qwen3-jax-2k-cluster-continuity](../../../../hypotheses/qwen3-jax-2k-cluster-continuity.md):
v007's 667 ms / 48,758 TPS / 31.4% MFU reproduces within ±2% on charles-v6e.
PASS also validates the new-cluster plumbing (cross-project image pull,
cross-region GCS write, xpk dispatch).

**Mechanism**: none — recipe identical to v007 (bs2 + splash + scoped-vmem
81920; scan/CE/remat/io-layout all default-off on trunk b991d9f).

**Predicted signal**: step ≈ 667 ms; op profile ≈ v007's (conv fusion ~38%,
collectives ~30%, splash ~8%); loss series in v007's band (12.08–12.10
descending).
