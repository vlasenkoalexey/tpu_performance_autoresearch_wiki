# Qwen3-8B jax v029 — bs5 @ seq8192 probe

Last rung of the batch ladder: [v027](2026-06-12-v027-bs4-mtfl.md) measured
23.72 GiB at bs4 (slope ≈ 4.06 GiB/bs) → bs5 projects 27.8 GiB (89% of
cap). Launch-only.

## Hypothesis under test

[qwen3-jax-bs5-probe](../../../../hypotheses/qwen3-jax-bs5-probe.md): bs5
fits and beats 6,040 tok/s/chip by > 1% (> 6,100). Counter-signals: bs3→bs4
gave only +1.8%, and the async-done bucket scales super-linearly (1.36×) —
bs5 may be flat-to-negative even if it fits.

**Diff vs v027**: `--batch_size=5`. Nothing else.

**Falsification criterion**: OOM, or ≤ 6,100 → refuted; batch axis closed
at bs4 on this substrate either way (this is a boundary measurement).

## Setup

- Hardware: v6e-8, fsdp=8, tp=1, 1 slice of `alekseyv-tpu-v6e8-spot-xpk`.
- Image: `qwen3-8b-jax:v6e8-qwen3-8b-jax-20260612-v024-offload` (= latest).
- Workload: `alekseyv-qwen3-cc5-jax-v029-bs5`.
- Single phase: bs5 seq8192 (327,680 tok/step), 20 steps, profiled 12–14,
  HLO dump; 25-flag stack verbatim; splash blocks at default (2048/1024).

## Results

20 steps clean, exit 0, **no OOM** (fits), no NaN, monotone loss 12.1032 →
12.0439. Steady spread 17.3 ms (< 0.3%).

| Metric | v027 (bs4) | **v029 (bs5)** | delta |
|--------|------------|-----------------|-------|
| Step time (median) | 5,425.0 ms (262,144 tok) | **6,815.5 ms (327,680 tok)** | +25.6% time for +25% tokens |
| tok/s/chip | 6,040 | **6,010** | **−0.5%** |
| MFU | 34.6% | 34.5% | −0.1 pp |
| vs PASS bar 6,100 | — | **FAIL (−1.5%)** | — |

Near-perfectly linear scaling — the remaining sub-linear buckets are fully
amortized by bs4; bs5 adds nothing.

## Verdict

**refuted** — fits but 6,010 ≤ 6,100 bar (−0.5% vs bs4, within noise of
flat). **The batch axis is closed at bs4** on this substrate: bs1→bs3

frontier.
