# Qwen3-8B jax v049 — activation sharding constraints

Inventory #4: `with_sharding_constraint(P("fsdp",None,None))` on the hidden
states at layer entry, attention output, MLP output, and layer exit
(env-gated `JAX_ACT_SHARDING=1`). The companion S item `param_scan_axis=1`
was assessed NOT cleanly S-effort on our lax.scan structure (needs a
per-step moveaxis copy) and is dropped — noted for the record.

[qwen3-jax-activation-shardings](../../../../hypotheses/qwen3-jax-activation-shardings.md):
PASS > 6,840 tok/s/chip (frontier +1%; step < 2,395 ms). Loss must match
12.1029/12.0995/12.0944 ±0.001 (constraints are semantics-free; CPU smoke
0-diff).
