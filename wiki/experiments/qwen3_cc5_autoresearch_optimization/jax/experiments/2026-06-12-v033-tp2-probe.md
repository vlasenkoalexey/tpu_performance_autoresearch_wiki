# Qwen3-8B jax v033 — TP=2 probe

First tensor-parallelism experiment on this lane (retrospective #2
direction #2; axis had zero coverage in 33 experiments). Launch-only:
`--tp_parallelism=2` on the v027 frontier config.

## Hypothesis under test

[qwen3-jax-tensor-parallelism](../../../../hypotheses/qwen3-jax-tensor-parallelism.md):
fsdp4×tp2 restructures the per-layer weight all-gathers (624 ms exposed
wait at fsdp8) enough to beat 6,100 tok/s/chip. Negative prior: llama3-jax
TP=2 = −14% (pre-flag-stack era, different model).

**Diff vs v027**: `--tp_parallelism=2` (mesh becomes fsdp4×tp2; global
batch 16 instead of 32; tokens/step 131,072). Crash/OOM = valid result
(TP path exercise status on this trainer is itself unknown).

**Falsification criterion**: crash, OOM, or ≤ 6,100 tok/s/chip → refuted;
TP closed on this lane with a measured number.

- Hardware: v6e-8, 1 slice of `<your-cluster>`.

## Results

20 steps clean, exit 0, no OOM, no sharding errors — **the full stack
(splash + chunked CE + offload_attn + scan) is TP-mesh-compatible**, an
engineering fact previously unknown. Loss descent clean (12.1029 → 12.0588
at global batch 16). Step spread < 1 ms.

| Metric | v027 (fsdp8) | **v033 (fsdp4×tp2)** | delta |
|--------|---------------|------------------------|-------|
| tokens/step | 262,144 | 131,072 (global batch 16) | −50% |
| Step (median 2–19) | 5,425.0 ms | **3,551.4 ms** | — |
| tok/s/chip | 6,040 | **4,613** | **−23.6%** |
| MFU | 34.6% | 26.5% | −8.1 pp |
| vs PASS bar 6,100 | — | **FAIL (−24.4%)** | — |

The step time at HALF the tokens is 65% of v027's — the tp-axis activation
collectives plus halved per-chip arithmetic intensity dominate any saving
from smaller fsdp weight gathers.

projection (`replica_groups={{0,1},{2,3},{4,5},{6,7}}`) — no collective
matmul (disabled by our own lane-standard flag), i.e. the most expensive
possible tp lowering. Even granting a better lowering, the 20.2% collective
floor dwarfs the 11.5% AG bucket it was meant to shrink.

## Verdict

**refuted** — falsification criterion met by a wide margin (4,613 ≤ 6,100).
The TP axis is now CLOSED on this lane with a measured number (−23.6%),
replacing the borrowed llama3-jax prior (−14%). Mechanism: at bs4/chip the
matmuls are already near the v6e arithmetic-intensity cliff; tp2 halves the
per-chip work while adding per-layer tp collectives — both effects negative
at this op-point. Keep the compatibility finding: tp>1 works mechanically
on this trainer if a future topology (multi-slice, larger model) needs it.
