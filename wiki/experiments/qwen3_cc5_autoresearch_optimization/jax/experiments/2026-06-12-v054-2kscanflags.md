# Qwen3-8B jax v054 — scan + 21-flag stack at 2k

Re-dispatch of [v053](2026-06-12-v053-2kflags.md) (compile-budget kill on the
unrolled graph) on the **scan substrate**: `--use_scan=True` +
`JAX_SCAN_UNROLL=1` + the 21-flag stack, vs the
[v052](2026-06-12-v052-2kbase.md) baseline (667.6 ms / 49,090 TPS / ≈31.6%
MFU).

## Hypothesis under test

[qwen3-jax-2k-frontier-port](../../../../hypotheses/qwen3-jax-2k-frontier-port.md),
flags lever on the scan substrate.

**Mechanism**: 21-flag stack targets the 31.7% collective bucket
(windowed-einsum ring confirmed ACTIVE in v052's HLO — its disable was
−341 ms at 8k); scan is the compile-feasibility substrate (step-parity at 8k,
v020; untested at 2k).

**Predicted signal**: collective buckets shrink materially; ring CP-start
instances vanish from HLO; peak HBM drops (27.34→19.66 GiB at 8k — if
reproduced, opens the 2k batch ladder per the
[HBM observation](../../../../observations/qwen3-2k-v6e8-hbm-headroom.md)).
PASS = tok/s/chip ≥ +3% vs v052 (step ≤ ~648 ms), loss parity. If flat/worse:
ambiguous between scan-cost and flag-no-op at 2k → disambiguate with a
scan-only control before closing the flag axis.

## Verdict

**inconclusive** for the hypothesis (flags at 2k — still untested);
**decisive substrate finding**: scan-without-remat does NOT fit at 2k/bs2
(filed as [observation](../../../../observations/scan-without-remat-oom.md)).
Consistent with the 8k campaign, where scan was always paired with a remat
policy (offload_attn → save_attn → save_qkv → save_qkv_ctx_hbm). Profile
section omitted: crash before step 0. Re-dispatch =
[v055](2026-06-12-v055-2kstack.md): scan + `save_qkv_ctx_hbm` +
`SPLASH_RESIDUAL_CKPT_NAME=context` + 21-flag stack — the certified 8k
recipe (minus io layout) at the 2k op-point.
