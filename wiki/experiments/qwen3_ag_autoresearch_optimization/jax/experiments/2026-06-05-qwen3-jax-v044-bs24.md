# v044: Batch Size 24 Scaling

## Hypothesis under test
- **Mechanism**: Increasing per-chip batch size to 24 (`global_batch=192`).
- **Predicted signal**: MFU increases due to higher arithmetic intensity and better amortization of host-device overheads compared to bs=16, without hitting the OOM encountered at bs=32.

## Results
- **Outcome**: `RESOURCE_EXHAUSTED` (XLA:TPU compile permanent error).
- **Details**: `Used 38.06G of 31.25G hbm. Exceeded hbm capacity by 6.82G.`

## Verdict
**REFUTED**. `bs=24` at `seq=2048` requires ~38GB of HBM for compilation, exceeding the 31.25GB limit on v6e. The limit for sequence length 2048 on this setup is likely `bs=16`.
