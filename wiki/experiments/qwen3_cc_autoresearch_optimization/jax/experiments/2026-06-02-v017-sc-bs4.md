# Qwen3-8B jax v017 — splash bs=4 + SparseCore offload (retry at scale)

## Hypothesis under test

**Hypothesis**: SparseCore collective-offload — which REGRESSED at the tiny bs=1
baseline ([v003](2026-06-02-v003-sc-offload.md), −4 pp) — may now **help** on the
v008 frontier shape (splash, bs=4): with a bigger, busier step the collective is a
larger absolute cost and offloading it off the TensorCore frees more matmul cycles
(this is the regime where it was +3.4% on the llama3 jax lane).

**Mechanism**: v008 frontier config + the three
`xla_tpu_enable_sparse_core_collective_offload_*` flags in `LIBTPU_INIT_ARGS`
(alongside the vmem flag). `--use_remat --use_splash --batch_size=4 --seqlen=2048`,
image `qwen3-8b-jax:v006-splash`. Flag-only.

**Predicted signal**: collective TC time-share drops; MFU > 32.4%.

**Falsification criterion**: no improvement / regression vs 32.4% (offload overhead
still > TC relief even at bs=4).
