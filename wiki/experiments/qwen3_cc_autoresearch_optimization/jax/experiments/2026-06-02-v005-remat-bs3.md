# Qwen3-8B jax v005 — remat + bs=3

## Hypothesis under test

**Hypothesis**: With remat the baseline (v004 = bs=2 @ 22.0%), bs=3 (global 24)
fits and lifts MFU further via higher MXU occupancy.

**Mechanism**: Flag-only `--use_remat=True --batch_size=3` on the v004-remat image, seq2048.

**Predicted signal**: fits; MFU > 22.0%.

**Falsification criterion**: OOM, or MFU ≤ 22.0%.

## Setup

- v6e-8 (fsdp=8, tp=1), image `qwen3-8b-jax:v004-remat`, `--use_remat=True --batch_size=3 --seqlen=2048`.
- global_batch 24, per-chip 3, bf16, 20 steps.

## Baseline comparison

| Metric | baseline bs=1 | v004 remat bs=2 | **v005 remat bs=3** |
|--------|---------------|-----------------|----------------------|
| Throughput/chip | 3,994 | 4,280 | **4,874** |
| MFU | 20.5% | 22.0% | **25.1%** |
| MXU util (xprof) | 19.9% | 27.8% | **32.7%** |

**+14% tok/s/chip over v004; +22% over baseline.** Batch keeps paying as long as it fits.
