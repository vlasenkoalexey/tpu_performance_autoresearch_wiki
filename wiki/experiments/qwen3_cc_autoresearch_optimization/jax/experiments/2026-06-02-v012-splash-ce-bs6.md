# Qwen3-8B jax v012 — remat+splash+CE bs=6

## Hypothesis under test

**Hypothesis**: Stacking remat + splash + tokamax CE frees enough HBM for bs=6, pushing MFU past the splash-bs4 frontier (32.4%).

**Mechanism**: `--use_remat --use_splash --use_tokamax_ce --tokamax_ce_impl=mosaic_tpu --batch_size=6` + vmem flag, image `qwen3-8b-jax:v010-ce`.

| Cause | tokamax mosaic_tpu CE op's lazy absl config parse collides with fire argv |
