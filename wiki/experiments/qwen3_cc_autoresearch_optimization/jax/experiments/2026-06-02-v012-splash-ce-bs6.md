# Qwen3-8B jax v012 — remat+splash+CE bs=6

## Hypothesis under test

**Hypothesis**: Stacking all three memory wins — remat + splash (no N² scores) +
tokamax CE (no `[B,L,V]` logits) — frees enough HBM for bs=6 (global 48), pushing
MFU past the splash-bs4 frontier (32.4%) via still-higher occupancy.

**Mechanism**: `--use_remat --use_splash --use_tokamax_ce --tokamax_ce_impl=mosaic_tpu
--batch_size=6 --seqlen=2048` + `--xla_tpu_scoped_vmem_limit_kib=98304`, image
`qwen3-8b-jax:v010-ce` (has remat+splash+CE). CE removes the ~1.9 GB/chip logits
that would otherwise cap batch.

**Predicted signal**: fits (no OOM); loss ~12 stable; MFU > 32.4%.

**Falsification criterion**: OOM (bs=6 still too big — try bs=5), NaN loss (CE bug),
or MFU ≤ 32.4% (occupancy saturated).
