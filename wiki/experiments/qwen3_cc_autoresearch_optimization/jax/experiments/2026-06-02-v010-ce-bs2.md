# Qwen3-8B jax v010 — tokamax CE (canary, bs=2)

## Hypothesis under test

**Hypothesis**: The streamed tokamax cross-entropy (chunked_xla) is numerically
correct (loss ~12 stable) and at least neutral on MFU vs remat-bs2 (22.0%) — the
canary that validates the CE kernel before stacking it with splash for bigger batch.

**Mechanism**: `--use_tokamax_ce=True --tokamax_ce_impl=chunked_xla` routes the loss
through `tokamax.linear_softmax_cross_entropy_loss` under a shard_map (fp32 boundary
cast, psum over fsdp), skipping the `[B,L,V]` logit materialization. remat ON, bs=2,
seq2048, NO splash (isolates CE). Image `qwen3-8b-jax:v010-ce`.

**Predicted signal**: loss ~12 stable (CE correctness); MFU ≈ 22.0% (CE is a memory
lever, ~neutral on speed at a shape that already fits); peak HBM lower (no logits).

**Falsification criterion**: NaN/garbage loss (CE mis-wired), crash, or a large MFU
regression.
